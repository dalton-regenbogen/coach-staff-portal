ONE TEAM. ONE VISION. ONE FAMILY.

What Makes Brookside... Brookside?

We're more than a swim team. We're a movement of mentorship, memory-making, and meaningful growth—both in and out of the water.

Our Vision

Our vision goes beyond the pool. We want to be known as a community that shapes not only resilient, kind, and ambitious athletes but also cultivates a supportive environment for our staff and families. We see a future where everyone involved takes the experiences and values they've gained here to make a positive impact in their own lives and in their communities. We're striving for a day where we're not just a team, but a family, united in never settling for less than our dreams.

The Mission

We're all about inspiring our swimmers, our staff, and our families to grow together. Our mission is to foster an environment that promotes personal growth, kindness, and ambition. We aim to empower everyone involved to believe in their potential and chase their dreams. We're one staff, one team, one family, committed to supporting each other and nurturing a strong sense of community.

The Values That Keep Us Aligned

Our Core Values are the heartbeat of Brookside. They guide how we coach, how we lead, and how we support one another—whether we’re running a practice, leading a cheer, or checking in after a tough race.They’re not slogans. They’re standards.

Ambition

We promote a culture of ambition, motivating our swimmers and staff to set high goals and work diligently towards achieving them.

Communication

We value clear and open communication. We believe that effective teamwork thrives on honest, direct, and constructive dialogues.

Community

We are more than a team, we are a community. We foster a strong sense of belonging, unity, and camaraderie among all team members.

Growth

We believe in the continuous pursuit of personal and professional growth, instilling this value in both our swimmers and our staff. We encourage an environment of learning, curiosity, and exploration.

Kindness

We strive to treat each other with respect, understanding, and empathy. Our team is a safe place where everyone feels valued and supported.
